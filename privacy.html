Who we are

Imperial College Cycling Club (ICCC) is a student-run sports club affiliated with Imperial College Union (ICU), operating at Imperial College London.

Imperial College Union is a registered charity (Charity No. 1151241) and the legal body under which ICCC operates. Any formal queries regarding the club should be directed to the Union.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not set any cookies, tracking pixels, or local storage identifiers. No analytics, advertising scripts, or third-party trackers are loaded.

Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN, which may result in your IP address being transmitted to Google as part of the font request. This is standard browser behaviour for externally hosted fonts and is the only third-party connection made by this site. No personal data is stored or processed by ICCC as a result of this.

Personal data

This website does not collect personal data directly. We do not operate contact forms, account registration, or any data collection mechanism on this site.

If you contact us by email or through Instagram, your contact details are used solely to respond to your enquiry and are not shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes.

Club membership is managed through the Imperial College Union membership system. Please refer to ICU's privacy policy for details on how membership data is handled.

UK GDPR

Where ICCC processes any personal data (for example, managing a club mailing list or organising events), we do so in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

The lawful basis for processing member contact information is legitimate interest — specifically, to communicate club activities, ride schedules, and events to members who have joined the club.

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time where consent is the basis for processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cycle@imperial.ac.uk.

Hosting & infrastructure

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., a subsidiary of Microsoft). GitHub may log IP addresses and request metadata as part of standard server operation. Please refer to GitHub's privacy statement for details.

ICCC has no access to server logs or visitor data.

Photography & media

Photos published on this website are taken during club activities. By participating in ICCC events, members implicitly consent to being photographed for club use, including publication on this website and on the club's Instagram account (@iccycling).

If you appear in a photo on this site and would like it removed, please contact us at cycle@imperial.ac.uk and we will action your request promptly.

External links

This website contains links to external sites including Instagram, the Imperial College Union, and other third-party services. ICCC is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before interacting with them.

Questions

If you have any questions about this notice or about how ICCC handles your data, please contact us at cycle@imperial.ac.uk.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
